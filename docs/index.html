Piman

The day of the announcement after watching Kanata making that little cute Ina's mii I went to Kaichou's stream excited to watch the big announcement! I was hoping for a birthday party or something like that, but I ended crying like the little girl that I used to be, more than what I cried since a long long time ago. I cried all the night, even If I knew that she wasn't going to die I just couldn't stop crying. And I didn't stopped until Kanata's stream about the subject was over, that little angel gave me the courage to go get a little sleep until Kaichou's stream. I tried my best to cheer up like she said, but even after that just scrolling in my home page in any social network made my tears flow again, I was hurt and I didn't have someone close to me to ask for a hug without expecting them to laugh at me 'cause I was crying for losing someone that in their eyes, is not real. So like that I passed that couple of days until the mengen stream about the subject, and I can't say why, but after that I finally felt some peace and I started to think positive, she wasn't gone yet, and even if it was just two weeks until the grad I decided that I was going to enjoy those days to the maximum amount posible. So right now, I'm going to enjoy these last days the most that I can, laugh, smile a lot, and comment more than I've ever done before, while trying to not lose a single minute of every stream that's left until the last day, because I once thought that there will always be a new stream tomorrow, but now that I have a time limit, I'll live with no regrets, and I'll cry my soul while trying to smile the day of the grad, cause I think that in the end, that's everything that she always wanted, to make us smile. Kiryu-kai is forever my bros, and Kaichou will always be in our hearts pushing us to move forward with our brightest smile.
